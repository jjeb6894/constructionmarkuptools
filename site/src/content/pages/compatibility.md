# Compatibility

Construction Markup Tools should organise Bluebeam Revu resources by trade, buyer intent, compatibility, and workflow. The site should make it easy to compare symbol packs, download a free sample, understand licensing, and choose the right bundle without reading through generic product grids.

## Recommended sections

- Trade-led category cards
- Free sample CTA
- Compatibility badges
- Bundle comparison grid
- Practical FAQs
- Clear licence and support links
- Bluebeam trademark disclaimer
