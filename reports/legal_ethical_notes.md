# Legal and Ethical Crawling Notes

This project is designed for lawful competitive intelligence and original site planning.

- Crawl public pages only.
- Respect `robots.txt`, canonical URLs, rate limits, and terms of service.
- Do not bypass logins, CAPTCHAs, Cloudflare, paywalls, checkout flows, or access controls.
- Do not scrape customer data, account data, private files, gated downloads, or paid assets.
- Do not download or reuse competitor `.btx`, `.zip`, PDF, image, symbol, or product files.
- Store raw HTML only as local evidence for audit and extraction.
- Use generated content as original drafts based on structured market signals and planned product data.
- Keep source traceability for every market insight.
- Treat all final commercial copy, product files, screenshots, and downloadable assets as assets that must be created originally.
